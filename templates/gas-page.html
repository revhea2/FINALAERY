GENERAL ACADEMIC STRAND (GAS)

Designed to prepare students who are more inclined to general areas of study rather than specialized fields, GAS will present the option for SHS students to take their electives from specialized subjects of any other strands.

Unlike other strands, the senior high school GAS gives students a more general overview on the different subjects rather than a focused approached. Students will tackle subjects from each of the different strands (HUMSS, STEM, and ABM).

The GAS gives senior high school students exposure a variety of different subjects, allowing them to explore each one to see which will be the best fit for them
